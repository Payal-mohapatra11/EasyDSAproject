Theory: Linked List Operations

Linked lists store elements in nodes with pointers/references to the next (and previous in doubly linked lists) node.

  • Insert at Head: O(1) time, O(1) space
  • Insert at Tail (SLL): O(n) time, O(1) space
  • Insert at Tail (DLL with tail): O(1) time, O(1) space
  • Delete Head: O(1) time, O(1) space
  • Delete Tail (SLL): O(n) time, O(1) space
  • Delete Tail (DLL): O(1) time with tail pointer
  • Search: O(n) time, O(1) space
  • Overall storage: O(n) space for n nodes
